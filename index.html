About Our School

A safe, inspiring space for children to grow at their own pace.

Sweppa Kiddies Montessori is committed to delivering quality early childhood education using the Montessori approach. Our classrooms are carefully prepared environments where children learn through hands-on exploration, guided by trained educators who respect each child's unique developmental journey.

We believe every child has the potential to become a confident, independent, and compassionate learner. Our programme blends academic readiness with creative expression, social skills, and physical development.

Our Programmes

Tailored learning paths for every stage of early childhood development.

👶

Infant Community

A gentle introduction to structured learning for our youngest learners. Sensory play, motor skill development, and guided exploration in a warm, safe environment.

Ages 1 – 2 years
🎨

Toddler Programme

Building independence through practical life activities, language development, and creative expression. Children learn to care for themselves and their environment.

Ages 2 – 3 years
📚

Casa dei Bambini

The heart of Montessori education. A mixed-age classroom covering practical life, sensorial, mathematics, language, and cultural studies through hands-on materials.

Ages 3 – 5 years
🎓

Kindergarten Readiness

Preparing children for the transition to primary school with advanced literacy, numeracy, social readiness, and structured group learning activities.

Ages 5 – 6 years

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from parents and guardians.

Montessori education is a child-centred approach developed by Dr Maria Montessori. It emphasises hands-on learning, self-directed activity, and collaborative play. Children work with specially designed materials at their own pace, guided by trained educators who observe and support each child's individual development.
We welcome children from 1 year through 6 years of age. Our programmes are structured across four levels: Infant Community (1-2), Toddler Programme (2-3), Casa dei Bambini (3-5), and Kindergarten Readiness (5-6).
School runs from 7:30 AM to 12:30 PM for half-day learners, and 7:30 AM to 3:30 PM for full-day learners. An optional after-school care programme is available until 5:00 PM.
Start by completing the enquiry form on this website or contacting us via WhatsApp. We will invite you for a school visit, after which you can submit an application form with the required documents. Our admissions team will guide you through the process.
Yes. Full-day learners receive a balanced mid-morning snack and a nutritious lunch. We accommodate dietary requirements where possible. Half-day learners receive a snack only. Parents may also send packed meals if preferred.
We use a combination of this website's bulletin board, direct WhatsApp messaging, printed circulars, and scheduled parent-teacher meetings. Urgent notices are sent via WhatsApp. Termly progress reports are shared with each family.
